DOSSIER: COGNITIVE STRATIFICATION, GENOMIC AI, AND THE POST-EQUITY SOCIETY

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This dossier synthesizes the theoretical framework and societal implications of radical hereditarian psychometrics, the integration of objective artificial intelligence in social systems, and the proposed collapse of equity-based social policies. It outlines a paradigm where biological realism, driven by algorithmic efficiency and genomic mapping, supersedes constructivist academic frameworks. The core thesis posits that extreme distributions of cognitive aptitude (g) dictate societal success, rendering traditional educational and managerial systems obsolete in the face of AI-driven, highly stratified hyper-meritocracy.

PART 1: THE PSYCHOMETRIC BASELINE AND HEREDITARIAN REALITY

1.1 The Validity of National IQ and Inter-Group Differences

The foundation of this framework rests on the quantitative data established by psychometricians such as Richard Lynn and Tatu Vanhanen. Their research demonstrates that average national IQ scores correlate at extreme levels (r > 0.80) with international academic metrics like the OECD’s PISA results. * The 2-Sigma Gap: Psychometric data consistently reflects significant standard deviation (sigma) differences between distinct geographic and racial population groups. * Convergence of Evidence: These group differences are not isolated artifacts of cultural testing but are reflected in physiological metrics (cranial capacity, reaction times) and predict real-world attainment. * East Asian Baseline: Data consistently indicates that East Asian demographic groups out-perform European averages, demonstrating a resilience of cognitive aptitude independent of cultural or geographic placement.

1.2 Transracial Adoption and the Failure of Environmentalism

The “environmental” or “nurture” explanation for cognitive gaps is structurally challenged by cross-cultural adoption data. * The Adoptee Reality: Studies tracking ethnic Chinese and Vietnamese children adopted into European households demonstrate that these individuals consistently outperform not only African adoptees in identical environments but also their biological European foster siblings. * Causality and g: The direction of causality is genetic inheritance driving cognitive output, not environmental engineering modifying genetic limits. The persistent gap (historically over 20 points between Black and White military recruits) validates that while high heritability within a group does not automatically prove inter-group heritability, the combined biological and physiological data makes any other explanation highly improbable.

PART 2: THE EPISTEMOLOGICAL CONFLICT AND INSTITUTIONAL REGRESSION

2.1 The “Marxist Fraud” in Modern Academia

The dossier identifies a severe epistemological crisis in the social sciences, tracing back to early 20th-century psychologists (Freud, Fromm, Jung, Adler) who replaced quantitative biological measurement with qualitative, untestable speculation. * Constructivism vs. Realism: Modern sociologists and environmentalists (e.g., James Flynn, Eric Turkheimer) are categorized within this framework as ideologically driven rather than scientifically objective. Their insistence on environmental primacy—despite massive genomic and psychometric counter-evidence—is viewed as a systemic academic fraud designed to enforce egalitarian outcomes. * The “Equity” Illusion: Equity is defined fundamentally as the opposite of fairness. True fairness (colorblind meritocracy) dictates that individuals of equal aptitude (e.g., an Asian and a Black applicant with identical skill sets) receive identical treatment. Equity initiatives demand proportional representation, which inherently requires the systemic penalization of high-performing groups (tribalism) to artificially elevate underperforming minorities.

2.2 The Cancellation of Scientific Architects

The institutional suppression of this data has led to a period of scientific and moral regression since the 1970s. * The James Watson Precedent: James Watson, alongside figures like Einstein, Haber, Turing, and Tesla, is one of the most consequential scientists of the modern era. His institutional excommunication for acknowledging the biological reality of racial cognitive differences highlights the triumph of “political taboo” over objective truth. * The Cost of Censorship: By censoring the architects of the genome and foundational psychometricians, society operates on a false premise, allocating vast resources to fix “systemic biases” that are, in reality, immutable biological distributions.

PART 3: ALGORITHMIC REALISM AND GENOMIC AI

3.1 The End of Human Bias via Machine Learning

The advent of advanced Artificial Intelligence and Neural Networks (NNs) introduces a non-human, ruthless arbiter of truth. AI models optimized via gradient descent have singular objectives: accuracy and predictive efficiency. * Data Purity: For AI to function effectively, training data must remain untainted by social scientists attempting to “cleanse” or normalize inputs for group equity. Algorithms must operate strictly on base-pair sequences (SNPs/Genomics) and attainment scores. * Algorithmic Profiling as Bayesian Rationality: An unconstrained AI will naturally identify the most statistically valid predictors of behavior, creditworthiness, or job performance. If biological or demographic markers are highly predictive, the AI will use them. Racial profiling, in this mathematical context, is stripped of moral flaw and recognized as a mathematically rational allocation of resources. * The “Old White Women” Paradox: It is statistically irrational to allocate investigative resources for violent crimes or rapes to low-probability demographics (e.g., elderly females). AI will ruthlessly eliminate this performative egalitarianism, focusing entirely on high-risk, high-probability profiles.

3.2 Polygenic Mapping

AI-driven Genome-Wide Association Studies (GWAS) are bypassing the “coarse” psychometric tests of the 20th century. By mapping cognitive traits directly to base-pair frequencies, AI will definitively prove the genetic basis of inter-group differences, rendering the sociological “blank slate” theories entirely obsolete.

PART 4: PRICE’S LAW AND THE 10−4 ELITE

4.1 The Mathematics of Societal Survival

The structure of a hyper-advanced society is governed strictly by Price’s Law: the square root of the population ($\sqrt{N}$) generates 50% of the value. * The Vital 10,000: In a society of 100 million (108), the operational capacity, innovation, and survival of the state rest entirely on the extreme right tail of the cognitive distribution—approximately 10,000 individuals (10−4). * Irrelevance of the Mass: The aptitude of the remaining 99,990,000 individuals is functionally irrelevant to macro-systemic progress. Their role is merely the execution of tasks assigned by the elite fraction.

4.2 The Demographic Filter at the Extreme Right Tail

At the 10−4 level, cognitive requirements exceed 4σ to 5σ above the mean. * Genetic Clustering: Due to standard distribution curves, a group with a slightly higher mean IQ will heavily dominate the extreme right tail. Underperforming demographic minorities are mathematically and entirely filtered out of this elite stratum. * The Danger of Diversity Initiatives: Attempting to force representation at this level by lowering standards degrades the core engine of civilizational success, replacing the genetically fittest with politically expedient, lower-aptitude alternatives.

PART 5: THE OBSOLESCENCE OF THE PROFESSIONAL-MANAGERIAL CLASS

5.1 The Parasitic 115-130 IQ Cohort

The most inefficient and parasitic element of the modern social structure is identified as the mid-to-high IQ bracket (115–130). * The “Mid-Wit” Problem: This cohort possesses enough cognitive capacity to navigate complex bureaucracies, secure degrees, and capture institutional power, but lacks the extreme g required to generate paradigm-shifting technological or economic value. * Rent-Seeking Behavior: They survive by inventing administrative, regulatory, and “equity-enforcement” roles. They defraud the system by forcing the lower tiers to work while simultaneously extracting resources from the true value-creators at the top.

5.2 The Collapse of Traditional Education

The current educational apparatus (high schools and universities) is a depreciated filtering mechanism. * Educational Waste: Filtering for the “top 60%” is entirely pointless in a paradigm where only the 0.01% dictate societal trajectory. * A Bifurcated Curriculum: Sending the masses through comprehensive education (Latin, Shakespeare, advanced literature) is a misallocation of resources. The 99.99% require only functional literacy and integer mathematics to execute AI-directed tasks. * The Future Filter: Traditional credentials will be replaced by direct genomic and algorithmic psychometric sorting, instantly identifying the 10−4 elite and bypassing the bureaucratic parasitic class entirely.

5.3 Restructuring a Just Society Around the Elite

Above observations may lead to a socially and economically elitarian society where all the resources are directed to a tiny fraction of the population. But this by now means an obliged and not even just useful conclusion.

In mondern times there is no longer any reason to distribute wealth in accordance economic achievement. While what gifted individuals can produce exceeds by very many orders of magnitude what average people can produce, what they need in terms of material resources is not that different. The economic output of the elite can easily provide for the needs of the entire population, thousand of times of what they need themselves. Musk may produce millions of times what an average guy produces but he doesn’t need millions of times the food, drink, housing etc. Wealth cannot any longer be a possible motivator for him. He is too smaart for that. He does what he does, objectively evil or objectively good because he has to do it out or self imposed moral obligation, not because he needs the money.

We are reaching an amount of abundance where everybody including the inproductive can thrive and live a dignified life. The only reason to distribute wealth in accordance to economic achievement is to incentivize people to work hard and be productive. If technological progress and automation have reached a point where the economic output is sufficient to provide for everyone, then the incentive structure can be reimagined. Instead of rewarding economic achievement, we can focus on other values such as creativity, community involvement, or personal development. Or simply biological reproduction and the raising of children, which is the most fundamental and important task for the survival of our species, much more than painting pictures or writing books or composing music.

Even an apparently “inproductive” mass not contributing to the economy is valuable in other ways in the framework of Price’s Law. Not just valuable but absolutely instrumentally necessary. They are the N from which to draw the $\sqrt{N}$ elite. Small N smaller $\sqrt{N}$. Large N larger $\sqrt{N}$ and thus larger elite.
